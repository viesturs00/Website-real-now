How We Work

An efficient workflow that converts your content into clean, ready-to-post videos.

01

Kickoff & Strategy Alignment

We begin with a focused call to clarify your goals, audience, and creative style. You share any raw footage, podcasts, or streams, and we outline a tailored plan for how your content will be transformed and distributed.

02

Content Breakdowns & Production

Our team reviews your material to pinpoint the strongest moments, hooks, and narratives. From there, we produce high-impact clips with captions, motion design, and platform-specific edits optimized for TikTok, Reels, Shorts, and more.

03

Launch & Distribution

Once approved, your campaign goes live. Our creator network (or your internal team, depending on your model) pushes content out across the major short-form platforms to generate rapid organic reach.

04

Performance Tracking & Iteration

You receive a real-time dashboard showing every clip, post, and view. We analyze what's working, refine future angles, and plan the next rollout so your momentum keeps building.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ClipWorks

+

ClipWorks is a short-form editing service that transforms your long-form content into polished, platform-ready clips for TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

How does your process work from start to finish?

+

You outline your objectives during a short kickoff call, share your footage, and we take over from there. Our team selects the strongest moments, produces high-performing edits, briefs our creator network, and oversees the entire distribution cycle.

What types of brands or creators do you support?

+

We work with a wide range of clients, including creators, founders, e-commerce brands, musicians, podcasters, and larger companies looking to expand their short-form presence. Any business aiming to grow through consistent, viral-ready content can benefit.

What does it cost to get started?

+

Budgets range from $500 to $5,000, depending on the scope of your content and the volume of clips you want produced. We build your plan around your goals, and during your intro call you'll receive a clear, transparent breakdown based on your posting needs.
